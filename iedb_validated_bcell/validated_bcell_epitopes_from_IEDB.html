Description

This track shows B-cell epitope sequences as annotated by National Institute for Allergy and Infectious Diseases (NIAID) Immune Epitope Database (IEDB). The epitopes with positive B-cell assays are displayed on this track. The fields of this track are synonymous to the fields displayed by IEDB data sheet upon exporting it.

EpitopeID is a clickable link to each epitope on IEDB site where details about assays and literature are provided.

References

Randi Vita, Swapnil Mahajan, James A. Overton, Sandeep Kumar Dhanda, Sheridan Martini, Jason R. Cantrell, Daniel K. Wheeler, Alessandro Sette, and Bjoern Peters. The Immune Epitope Database (IEDB): 2018 update. Nucleic Acids Research, January 2019.
